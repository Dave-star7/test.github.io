About Us

At Angle Point, we specialize in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Land Survey, and Remote Sensing services tailored to meet the needs of organizations and companies worldwide. Our expertise lies in leveraging cutting-edge technology to provide accurate, insightful, and actionable geospatial solutions.

What We Do

1. GIS and Remote Sensing Projects: We offer comprehensive services for GIS and Remote Sensing projects, utilizing advanced tools and techniques to analyze spatial data, create maps, and extract valuable insights.

2. Training Programs: Empower your team with the skills they need for data-driven decision-making. Our training programs cover GIS, Land survey technologies, Remote Sensing, and mobile data collection techniques, equipping participants with practical knowledge to tackle real-world challenges.
